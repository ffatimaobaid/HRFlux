# HRFlux Standard Company Policies

## 1. Working Hours
- The standard work week is Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM.
- A mandatory 1-hour lunch break is scheduled between 12:00 PM and 2:00 PM.
- Employees are expected to fulfill 40 hours of work per week.

## 2. Dress Code Policy
- **Business Casual:** Employees are expected to dress in business casual attire from Monday to Thursday. Examples include slacks, khakis, button-down shirts, blouses, and dresses.
- **Casual Friday:** Employees may wear casual clothing on Fridays, including jeans in good condition, t-shirts (without offensive graphics), and sneakers.
- **Client Meetings:** Formal business attire (suits, ties, formal dresses) is strictly required when meeting with external clients or partners.

## 3. Leave Policy
- **Annual Leave:** Employees accrue 1.5 days of annual leave per month (18 days per year).
- **Sick Leave:** Employees are entitled to 10 days of paid sick leave per calendar year. A medical certificate is required for sick leave exceeding 3 consecutive days.
- **Casual Leave:** 5 days of casual leave are provided per year for personal emergencies.

## 4. Work From Home (WFH)
- Employees may request up to 2 days of remote work per week, subject to manager approval.
- High-speed internet and an environment free from distractions are required when working from home.

## 5. Code of Conduct
- HRFlux maintains a zero-tolerance policy towards harassment, discrimination, or bullying.
- Company assets (laptops, phones) are strictly for professional use.
